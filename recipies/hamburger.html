How to make a hamburger

This recipie will how you how to make a hamburger
Ingredients:
  • Bun
  • Patty
  • Cheese
  • Lettuce
  • Toamato
  • Hamburger Sauce
Steps:
  1. Place patty on grill
  2. Flip and put cheese on top
  3. Cut open bun and put patty between
  4. Put vegetables and hamburger sauce on patty and enjoy
